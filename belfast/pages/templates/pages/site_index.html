The Belfast Group represented a new generation of poets and writers from Northern Ireland.

From Philip Hobsbaum's house the Group grew to include young talent from all across Northern Ireland. The most notable members of the Group include Paul Muldoon, Michael Longley, James Simmons, and Seamus Heaney. Read more about the Group and their literary influence.

Interactive networks and digital group sheets bring new light to the Belfast Group's influences.
